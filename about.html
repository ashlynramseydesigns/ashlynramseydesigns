Ashlyn Ramsey

I've always been the one to sit back and observe my family members, which leads me to say I've always been adamant about setting my path.

I graduated my High School in Charleston, SC, and ended up moving to Savannah, GA shortly after. For a while, I just couldn't decide what I wanted to do with my life. Then, one day I was watching a YouTube video that involved a drawing of a celebrity, which got me thinking, "What about graphic design?" I grabbed my computer and started searching for schools offering graphic design which is where I found my college F.I.R.S.T. Institue. After a few months, I decided to move to Wisconsin with my now fiance and family. Still attending F.I.R.S.T online. Growing up I was always around music and artsy things. Music has always been a passion of mine. Leading me to say that singing is my hobby and a big part of my life. Meeting new people growing up I was anxious and scared, I still am at times. Ever since I've become a student at F.I.R.S.T. that's brought me out of my shell. However, I do love a good stay-at-home in bed bingeing on my shows, and watching movies all day.
